ISRO's major milestones

1969

ISRO was established to harness space technology for India.

1971

Satish Dhawan Space Centre formed in Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh.

1975

India's first satellite, Aryabhatta was launched.

1992

ASLV was launched successfully.

1993

PSLV, ISRO's most reliable launcher carried out its first successful mission.

1999

PSLV started launching foreign satellites.

2001

GSLV was successfully launched.

2002

Kalpana-1 satellite was launched.

2008

Chandrayaan-1 was successfully launched. A Tricolour was hoisted on the moon!

2017

ISRO created history after successfully launching 104 satellites from Sriharikota Space Centre.

2019

Chandrayaan-2 was launched on July 15.

ISRO continues to create history!
